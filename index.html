What is Flood Control?

Posted by Admin on September 21, 2025

Flood control is the practice of managing water to prevent flooding in communities. It is important in reducing damage and protecting lives. This includes building dams, levees, drainage systems, and creating awareness in communities to prepare for natural disasters. By implementing these strategies, communities can reduce the risk of loss of property, resources, and even lives. Education about flood control also encourages younger generations to value preparedness and resilience in times of crisis.

Importance of Reading

Posted by Teacher Jane on September 20, 2025

Reading helps improve vocabulary, knowledge, and critical thinking skills. It opens doors to new ideas and perspectives. By reading regularly, students strengthen comprehension, enhance creativity, and develop empathy for others. Books and articles allow us to explore different cultures, histories, and innovations that shape our world. Furthermore, it supports academic success, as most learning materials require a strong foundation in reading to understand concepts effectively.

Cyberbullying Awareness

Posted by Student Council on September 18, 2025

Cyberbullying affects mental health and well-being. Awareness and education help prevent and address this issue effectively. Students should be taught how to report, block, and avoid harmful online interactions. Schools and families can work together to build safe online spaces, encouraging kindness and empathy. Every student deserves a secure environment where learning is free from fear and harassment. Promoting digital responsibility empowers young people to become respectful digital citizens.
